About Me

Dr. Amanda Smith is a professional paediatrician who is committed to giving kids and teenagers sensitive care. She has a great deal of experience in child health and blends professionalism with a kind approach to make her young patients feel at ease and supported. The goals of Dr. Smith's holistic approach are to support families as they navigate the stages of childhood development, resolve issues, and promote wellness. Her commitment to paediatrics goes beyond the clinic as she speaks up in the neighbourhood for the health and welfare of kids.
